ITZY First Japanese Album

On October 18, 2023, ITZY made a remarkable entrance into the Japanese music scene with "RINGO," their first Japanese album. Featuring the captivating title track "RINGO" and "Sugar-holic," the album offers 11 electrifying tracks, including fresh originals, Japanese versions, and pre-releases.
In just one week, "RINGO Album" sold an impressive 24,780 copies, securing a spot on the Billboard Japan charts
